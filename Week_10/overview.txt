This week we will focus on the object oriented design pattern "Composition and Aggregation".
We have informally worked with this approach in other labs but will formalize our study
with some important security concepts. Designing applications with composition is easy 
enough, but there are some crucial "under the hood" concepts that do require some dedicated attention.

In the lab you will revisit the beloved CS Food Truck, making it more object oriented

Be sure to do the reading and study the examples
